Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical landscaping project take?

Project duration varies depending on the scope and complexity. Small projects may take 1-2 weeks, while larger transformations can take 4-8 weeks or more.

Do you offer eco-friendly landscaping options?

Yes, we specialize in sustainable landscaping practices, including native plant selection, water-efficient irrigation, and organic lawn care methods.

Can you work with my existing landscape design?

Absolutely! We can enhance or modify your current landscape, or start from scratch if you prefer a complete redesign.

Do you provide maintenance services after installation?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance plans to keep your landscape looking its best year-round. We can customize a plan to fit your needs and budget.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the greater Portland metropolitan area, including surrounding suburbs within a 30-mile radius.
