Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you cater to?

We offer classes for all age groups, from children as young as 5 to adults of any age. Our courses are designed to accommodate different skill levels and interests.

Do I need to bring my own equipment or supplies?

For most classes, basic supplies are provided. However, for certain courses like music lessons, you may need to bring your own instrument. Specific requirements are listed in the course descriptions.

Are there any prerequisites for joining advanced classes?

Advanced classes usually require some prior experience or skills. We offer placement assessments to help determine the best level for you. You can also start with introductory classes and progress from there.

Do you offer trial classes or open days?

Yes, we regularly host open days where you can try out different classes and meet our instructors. We also offer single trial sessions for most of our regular courses at a reduced rate.

Is there a performance or exhibition opportunity for students?

Absolutely! We organize seasonal showcases, recitals, and exhibitions where students can display their work or perform. These events are a great way to celebrate creativity and share accomplishments with family and friends.
