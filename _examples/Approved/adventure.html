Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. For defective products, we provide a 1-year warranty.

Do you have a physical store?

Currently, we operate exclusively online. However, we're planning to open our first brick-and-mortar store next year.

Can I rent equipment instead of buying?

We don't offer rental services at the moment, but we're considering adding this option in the future.

Do you provide gear recommendations for specific trips?

Absolutely! Our customer service team includes experienced outdoor enthusiasts who can provide personalized gear recommendations based on your trip details and skill level.
